Each user has their own personalized dashboard where they can organize, track, and manage their tasks. This dashboard provides a centralized space for all your tasks, making it easy to stay on top of your responsibilities and deadlines.

To access your dashboard and start managing your tasks, simply visit the [Employee Pages](https://www.notion.so/ticketstatus/906b539e9e2f4aa58c4358e5f795a9d8?v=64e02da3a452454d8ffbe3306af93328).
